# Partnership & Channel Strategy

## Partnership Types

| Type | Description | Value | Example |
|------|-------------|-------|---------|
| **Marketing Partners** | Co-marketing, co-events, content swaps | Audience reach, credibility | Joint webinar with complementary tool |
| **Distribution Partners** | Resellers, VARs, MSPs, marketplaces | Revenue, market access | AWS Marketplace listing |
| **Technology Partners** | Integrations, ISVs, OEMs | Product value, sticky ecosystem | Zapier integration |
| **Services Partners** | SIs, agencies, consultants | Implementation, customer success | Accenture as implementation partner |
| **Referral Partners** | Affiliates, influencers, advocates | Low-CAC acquisition | Customer referral program |

## Partner Program Design

### Tiered Structure

| Tier | Criteria | Benefits | Commitment |
|------|----------|----------|-----------|
| **Registered** | Sign up, basic training | Deal registration, co-brand rights | Minimal |
| **Silver** | $[X] sourced ARR, certification | Lead sharing, MDF, listing | Quarterly reviews |
| **Gold** | $[X] sourced ARR, dedicated SE | Priority support, joint planning, events | Monthly reviews |
| **Platinum** | $[X]+ sourced ARR, executive sponsor | Custom programs, exclusive features | Strategic alignment |

### Partner Economics

```yaml
Revenue Share Models:
  Referral: 10-20% of first year ACV (one-time)
  Reseller: 20-40% margin on all deals
  Marketplace: 15-30% platform fee
  White-label: 50-60% of retail price

ROI Formula:
  Partner_ROI = (Partner_Influenced_ARR - Partnership_Costs) / Partnership_Costs × 100

Benchmarks:
  Partner-influenced revenue: 26-28% of total ARR (2026 benchmark)
  Partner-sourced revenue: 10-15% of total ARR
  Partner CAC: 40-60% lower than direct sales CAC
```

## Partner Selection Criteria

```yaml
Must-Have:
  - Shared target customer (same ICP, different product)
  - Complementary value (not competitive)
  - Aligned incentives (win-win economics)

Nice-to-Have:
  - Established customer base in target segment
  - Sales team that can position your product
  - Technical integration potential
  - Brand credibility in target market

Red Flags:
  - Overlapping product roadmap
  - Misaligned pricing (their enterprise vs your SMB)
  - No dedicated partner program team
  - History of partner conflicts
```

## Marketplace Strategy

### Platform Selection

| Marketplace | Best For | Fee | Pros | Cons |
|-------------|---------|-----|------|------|
| AWS Marketplace | Enterprise, infra | 15-20% | Procurement simplification, committed spend | Long listing process |
| Salesforce AppExchange | CRM-adjacent tools | 15-25% | Massive ecosystem | Salesforce dependency |
| HubSpot Marketplace | Marketing/sales tools | Free-15% | Growing ecosystem | Smaller than SFDC |
| Shopify App Store | E-commerce tools | 0-20% | Merchant access | Revenue share changes |
| VS Code / JetBrains | Developer tools | 0% | Developer reach | Free ecosystem norm |
