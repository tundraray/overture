# Content Marketing Strategy

## IDEAL Framework

| Phase | Action | Description |
|-------|--------|-------------|
| **I**dentify | Know your audience | Map content to buyer personas and funnel stages |
| **D**iscover | Find opportunities | Keyword research, content gaps, competitor analysis |
| **E**mpower | Activate authentic voices | Subject matter experts, customers, community |
| **A**ctivate | Distribute across channels | Multi-channel promotion and repurposing |
| **L**earn | Measure and iterate | Track performance, iterate on what works |

## Content-Funnel Mapping

| Funnel Stage | Content Type | Goal | Metric |
|-------------|-------------|------|--------|
| **Awareness (TOFU)** | Blog posts, social, infographics, podcasts | Attract and educate | Traffic, impressions, reach |
| **Consideration (MOFU)** | Case studies, webinars, comparison guides, whitepapers | Build trust and evaluate | Leads, email signups, time-on-site |
| **Decision (BOFU)** | Demos, free trials, ROI calculators, testimonials | Convert to customer | Trial signups, demo requests, conversion rate |
| **Retention** | Docs, tutorials, community, newsletters | Retain and expand | NPS, feature adoption, expansion revenue |

## E-E-A-T (Google Quality Standard)

| Signal | Description | How to Demonstrate |
|--------|-------------|-------------------|
| **Experience** | First-hand experience with the topic | Share real data, case studies, personal results |
| **Expertise** | Deep knowledge in the domain | Author bios, credentials, depth of content |
| **Authoritativeness** | Recognition by peers and industry | Backlinks, citations, mentions, awards |
| **Trustworthiness** | Accuracy, transparency, honesty | Sources cited, methodology shown, disclaimers |

**Rule**: AI-generated content without E-E-A-T signals will be outranked by content with genuine expertise markers.

## Content Cluster Strategy

### Pillar + Cluster Model

```
Pillar Page (broad topic, 3000+ words)
  ├── Cluster 1 (subtopic article, 1500+ words)
  ├── Cluster 2 (subtopic article, 1500+ words)
  ├── Cluster 3 (subtopic article, 1500+ words)
  └── Cluster N (subtopic article, 1500+ words)

Internal links: Every cluster links to pillar, pillar links to all clusters.
Effect: Builds topical authority with search engines.
```

## Content Repurposing

Maximize ROI by transforming content across formats:

```
Long-form article
  → Thread on X/LinkedIn (key points)
  → Short video (90-second summary)
  → Infographic (data points)
  → Podcast episode (discussion)
  → Email newsletter (highlights)
  → Slide deck (for webinar)
```

**Rule**: Create once, distribute 7+ times. One deep piece = 4-6 weeks of content.
